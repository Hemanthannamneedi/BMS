About Our Portal

The Budget Management Portal was created to provide student clubs with a modern, transparent, and efficient way to manage their finances. We understand the challenges of handling club funds, from tracking expenses to processing requests, and we built this platform to simplify every step of the process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower student leaders and treasurers with the tools they need to manage club finances with confidence. We aim to foster a culture of transparency and accountability, ensuring every member knows where their funds are going.

The Team

We are a small team of developers and student leaders who saw a need for a better solution. Our collective experience in both technology and club management drove us to create a portal that is not only powerful but also intuitive and user-friendly.

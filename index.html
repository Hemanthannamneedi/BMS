Key Features

Explore the powerful tools and functionalities of our Budget Management Portal.

Fund Requests

Members can easily request funds for their events and track the status of their requests through the system.

Member Management

Admins can add new members, approve their requests, and effectively manage all club members.

Expense Tracking

An easy-to-use dashboard is available to track all transactions and budget expenses, ensuring complete transparency.

Reporting

Comprehensive reports on the club's financial status can be easily generated and viewed.

Security

Top priority is given to the security of club information and data, so your data is always safe.

Collaboration

This portal promotes collaboration between club members and admins, allowing everyone to work together on one platform.
